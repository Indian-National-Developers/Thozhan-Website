100 Signals Traffic Awareness Campaign

100 Signals Event In Chennai

            At a time when Tamil Nadu continues to record the maximum accidents in the country, two non-profit organizations — Thozhan and V R Volunteers — plan to conduct a traffic awareness campaign at 100 traffic junctions in the city on a single day.

            The campaign will be held on August 10 at different traffic signals from 4.30pm to 6.30pm. Recent NCRB statistics show that Tamil Nadu has recorded the maximum deaths in the country in road accidents with 16,175 deaths in 67,757 accidents.

Few Snaps of 100 signals event

Highlights

  • The campaign aims to create awareness to wear helmets and seatbelts and also to ensure safe road crossing by insisting that pedestrians follow rules.
  • "We will also create awareness on importance of following the traffic signal.
  • It will also focus on drunken driving and highlight the risks associated with it," said a volunteer.
  • The campaign will also have short street plays on road safety and volunteers will distribute pamphlets with details on road safety and its importance.

    Accident Free Nation

  • Around 2000 wonderful citizens joined hands for the social cause of "Accident Free Nation" at 100 signals across chennai city.
  • More than a 1,00,000 nos bit notice carrying awareness messages were distributed across chennai city.
