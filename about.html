We are Passionate about

           The society will primarily work for the welfare of orphans, destitute children, and widows. However there is no bar to provide help to other sections of society. To grant financial aid or interest free loans to orphans, widows and poor children, old and infirm persons by way of maintenance, scholarship, books, uniforms, medical aid, clothes, food grains, medicines etc. or in such form as the society may decide. The executive have decided that the society will initially concentrate on proving help to widows and their children who have been left to fend to themselves due to unfortunate death of their husband and bread winner. Latter on we propose to undertake other activities such as providing help to their children to pursue their education, making them financially independent and general counseling. The members of the society according to our constitutions are all working in an honorary capacity and cannot draw any salary or honorarium. The members of the executive committee spend money from their own pocket for meeting even petty expenditure on traveling and petrol, tea etc...

Inspiration:

            A strong group of youth. Some of them are giving help to orphans, destitute children, and widows. This is the main inspiration in establishing this society as THOZHAN (means a new beginning); felt that with the active assistance of other like minded people they could serve the community more effectively. The other members are equally dedicated to this cause.

Monthly meet

Happens: Thozhan Office, Duration: 2 hours, Occurs: 1st Sunday Every month


  • * Getting provisional items and preparing some foods for the kids in Orphanage.
  • * Organizing events like speech computation, Quiz, Songs and dance programs.
  • * Organizing vacation programs like trip to a Theme Park or a resort.
  • * Educating and Creating awareness among those people in Orphanage.

Traffic Awarness Campign

Happens: T Nagar and Thiruvaanmaiyur, Duration: 2 hours, Occurs: 3rd and 4th Sunday Every month


  • * Place our banners near signal, which is written and Designed in order to obey Traffic Rules, in prior permission with Police.
  • * Preparing Ourselves in Masking or Face coloring, Getting ready with Pamphlets, Placards and Stickers
  • * Welcome Speech, with Mozhli Vaalthu, by the Leader who leads the Program on that Day
  • * Splitting out into teams leady by someone among us, and taking care of a particular signal
  • * Insisting People around us, to follow Traffic Rules, like asking them to were helmet, seat belt, making them to stop
        before the STOP Line and walk in the Zebra crossing.
  • * Assemble again for the taught sharing, and finishing with National Anthem
  • * Removing the displays, banners and return to regular duties.

Speech Workshop

Happens: Sivan Park KK Nagar, Duration: 2 hours, Occurs: 2nd Saturady Every month


  • * A topic on which the volunteer have to speak will be given as decided on monthly meet.
  • * Starting with the Mozhli Vaalthu and a welcome Speech.
  • * All the participants will be given some particular time to convey their Speech.
  • * Suggestions will be given at regular time after the completion of the Speech and corrections will be made.
  • * Concluded by National Anthem and the best Speaker.
