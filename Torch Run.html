"Safety not a Slogan,it's a way of life"

Marathon

                  Pheidippides, is said to have run from Marathon to Athens to deliver message of a victory to people from Marathon to Athens. We had planned to deliver the message of Road Safety and its importance across chennai city.

Safety Torch Run (65kms)

                  On account of Road safety week (2nd Jan’15-7th Jan’15), We had planned “Safety Torch Run”, a relay run with flame of light torch by individuals, who follow road safety rules on daily life, through critical spots of chennai to spread out the road safety messages to the people.

                  65Kms Relay run by Volunteers across chennai city for spreading out the road safety messages for bringing out the awareness among people. On 11.01.15, the Run started at 6 am at Valluvar Kottam and an ended at Ashok Pillar at 4 pm.

Safety Torch Run - 2015

The Event objective was to bring awareness on,

  • Importance of stop line and abiding Traffic signal.
  • Necessities if wearing helmet and seat belt.
  • Insisting Safe Road crossing by insisting pedestrians on following rules.
  • Risks of driving with distractions (Mobile phones, Music players, etc.,)
  • Dangers of drunken driving and risks associated with it.

    • Run Procedure

  • The 65kms distance was divided into Three Phases and 23 Junctions.
  • Before running, Every runner should take Pledge to follow traffic rules throughout their life and sign a commitment.
  • Each Junction has a group of three/four runners
  • To avoid disturbance or distraction to public, the run was a relay one, which means one person carries the torch and run from one point to another. Other 2 or3 runners follow the torch in cycle till his point reach.
